# 😴 Sleep Tracker App

A simple web-based sleep tracking and scheduling application to help you create better sleep habits and monitor your sleep patterns.

## Features

- **Create Sleep Schedules**: Set your ideal bedtime and wake-up time
- **Log Sleep Entries**: Record your actual sleep times and quality ratings
- **Track Sleep History**: View all your past sleep entries in a table
- **Sleep Statistics**: See your average sleep duration, quality, and total entries
- **Local Storage**: All data is saved on your device (no account needed)
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works on phones, tablets, and computers

## How to Use

1. **Open the App**: Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Create a Schedule**: 
   - Enter your ideal bedtime and wake-up time
   - Click "Create Schedule"
3. **Log Sleep**:
   - Select a date
   - Enter your actual bedtime and wake-up time
   - Rate your sleep quality (1-10)
   - Click "Log Sleep"
4. **View Statistics**: Your average sleep duration and quality will automatically update
5. **Clear Data**: Use the "Clear All Data" button to start fresh

- Your data is stored in your browser's local storage
- No account or login required
- Your data stays private on your device
